The von Fersen Family

This was a very well known Swedish (orignially Scottish) family and they had A LOT OF SEX. A lot of that sex was with Queens, Kings, Princes, Dutchesses and a whole host of other people. Axel von Fersen Jr. is the most well known in the U.S because of his affair with Queen Marie-Antionette, so most English written information is on him. Axel had affairs with 2 of the queens featured (Queen Marie Antionette and Queen Hedvig Elisabeth Charlotte). His other niece Ulrika von Fersen had an affair with Prince Fredrick Adolf of Sweden, King Charles/Carl's brother. Ulrika also had an affair with her cousin Sophie von Fersen's husband.
